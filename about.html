About Mopar Customs by Luther

Mopar is the official parts and accessories brand of all the brands under the Chrysler umbrella – Jeep, Ram, Dodge and Chrysler. But we're about more than parts – we're about people. Since XX, Mopar Customs by Luther has lived this promise, helping our customers make their vehicles exactly like they want them.

We are located inside Luther Brookdale Chrysler-Jeep-Dodge Ram in Brooklyn Park, Minnesota. We have thousands of parts on site and full-service bays equipped with the latest tools to help us perform customizations on vehicles, whether new off our lot or from your garage, efficiently and to the highest standard. With XX trained pros on staff, we can do whatever you have in mind. Plus we are backed by Luther, one of the region's top automotive names.

Since 1952, thousands of satisfied customers and businesses have trusted Luther for their vehicle needs. Whether purchasing a vehicle from one of our many convenient locations, or getting quality parts and service from Mopar Customs by Luther, the Luther name stands for dependability and quality.
